About Ukombozi Academy

Founded by Deliverance Church in Kenya, Ukombozi Academy stands as a beacon of Christian education, committed to nurturing young minds and hearts in accordance with biblical principles.

Our Vision

A caring center transforming young people with excellence to serve God and mankind

Our Mission

Offering high quality and holistic education based on christianity foundation.

Our Values

  • Spiritual Growth: Daily devotion, prayer, and biblical teaching
  • Integrity: Honesty, transparency, and moral uprightness in all we do
  • Community Service: Teaching students to serve others and make a positive impact
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in academics and character
  • Respect: Valuing each individual as created in God's image

Kenyan Curriculum Integration

We follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) framework, ensuring our students meet national educational standards while maintaining our Christian foundation. Our approach integrates faith-based learning with modern educational practices, preparing students for both academic success and spiritual maturity.

Curriculum & Academics

Our comprehensive educational program follows the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) while integrating Christian values and principles throughout the learning experience.

Early Years (Pre-Primary)

Ages 3-6 years

  • Play-based learning with Christian values
  • Language development (English & Kiswahili)
  • Basic numeracy and literacy
  • Creative arts and music
  • Physical development activities

Primary School (Grades 1-6)

Ages 6-12 years

  • Core subjects: Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Science
  • Social Studies and Religious Education
  • Creative Arts and Physical Education
  • Computer Studies and Life Skills
  • Daily devotion and Bible study

Junior Secondary (Grades 7-9)

Ages 12-15 years

  • Core subjects with specialization tracks
  • Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Sciences
  • Social Studies, Religious Education
  • Foreign Languages and Computer Studies
  • Leadership and character development

Co-Curricular Programs

Sports & Physical Education

Football, basketball, athletics, swimming, and other physical activities

Arts & Culture

Music, drama, visual arts, and cultural activities

Spiritual Development

Daily devotions, Bible study, prayer groups, and Christian fellowship

Clubs & Societies

Debate, science club, environmental club, and community service

Our Teaching Philosophy

Faith-Integrated Learning

Every subject is taught from a Christian perspective, helping students understand how their faith applies to all areas of life and learning.

Personalized Attention

Small class sizes ensure each student receives individual attention and support tailored to their unique learning needs and abilities.

Moral Development

Character education is woven throughout the curriculum, teaching values such as honesty, respect, responsibility, and service to others.

Admissions

Join the Ukombozi Academy family and give your child the gift of faith-based education combined with academic excellence.

Admission Process

1

Application

Complete the online application form or visit our school office to collect application materials.

2

Documentation

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and passport photos.

3

Assessment

Students may be required to take an assessment test to determine appropriate grade placement.

4

Interview

Parents and students meet with school administration to discuss expectations and answer questions.

5

Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete enrollment procedures and fee payment to secure your child's place.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical certificate
  • Parent/Guardian identification

Fee Structure

Our fees are competitive and include tuition, meals, and most educational materials. Financial assistance may be available for qualifying families. Please contact us for detailed fee information.
